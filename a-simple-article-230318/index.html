一篇简单的英语范文

2023年03月18日 544 字 大概 2 分钟

范文一: 鼓励学生培养创造力
Directions: For this part, you are allowed 30 minutes to write an essay on why students shouldbe encouraged to develop creativity.

In the contemporary society where technology is developing at a rapid rate, creative thinking has been brought into focus. At the same time, many experts suggest that both teachersand schools should attach great importance to encouraging students to develop creativity. Anumber of reasons can be collected to explain the suggestion.

The reasons why the development of students’ creativity is of considerable importance areas follows. Perhaps the primary reason is that developing a creative mindset is beneficial toacademic success. In addition, no one could deny the fact that creative ability can exert a positive influence on future career. The stories of most successful entrepreneurs in the world, such as Steve Jobs, provide a glimpse into how creative mind can help them find business opportunitiesand build a prosperous company. Lastly, with competition growing fiercely, thinking outside thebox is increasingly a requirement of ensuring more desirable results. Then how to develop creativity? Perhaps the first step is that students need to read extensively to enrich mind. Another crucial step is to put novel ideas into practice.

In conclusion, there are many well-documented benefits of being creative and I highlysuggest that everyone, especially university students invest effort to cultivate creativity.

译文:

在科技快速发展的当代社会，创造性思维成为人们关注的焦点。与此同时，许多专家建议老师和学校都应该重视鼓励学生发展创造力。许多原因可以来解释这一建议。

学生创造力的发展很重要的原因如下。也许最主要的原因是, 发展一种创造性的思维 方式有利学术成功。此外，没有人能否认这一事实，创造力可以对未来的职业产生积极的影响。世界上最成功的企业家的故事，如史蒂夫·乔布斯，可以让我们一窥创造性思维如何帮助他们找到商业机会并建立一个繁荣的公司。最后，随着竞争的日益激烈, 打破常规思维越来越是确保获得更理想结果的必要条件。 那么如何培养创造力呢? 也许第一步 是学生需要广泛阅读来丰富思想。另一个关键
步骤是将新想法付诸实践。

总之，有创造力有许多有据可查的好处，我强烈建议每个人, 尤其是大学生，努力培养创造力。
