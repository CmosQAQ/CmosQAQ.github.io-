英语范文二

范文二：平衡工作和休闲
Directions: For this part, you are allowed 30 minutes to write an essay on how to balance work and leisure. (2018.12.卷二)

With society developing at a rapid rate and pressure increasing steadily, recent years have witnessed the increase in the number of people who fail to balance work and leisure. In light of this situation, many experts suggest that great importance should be attached to achieving work-leisure balance, as it plays an essential role in living a happy and successful life.

One main reason why many people are unable to keep a balance between work and leisure nowadays is that the coupling of increased workloads with modern technologies has made it increasingly difficult for people to cut the connection to jobs in off-hours. Then how to reverse this situation? Perhaps the first step is that people need to improve working efficiency and time management ability. Specifically speaking, it is necessary to prioritize tasks and focus on the most important things either in the workplace or at home. Another crucial step is to establish boundaries between work and leisure time. Lastly, it is imperative for the media coverage to draw public attention to the balance between work and leisure.

In conclusion, we should not ignore the problem mentioned above and I highly suggest that everyone, especially young people invest effort to strike a balance between work and leisure.

译文:

随着社会的快速发展和压力的不断增加，近年来无法平衡工作和休闲的人越来越多。鉴于这种情况, 许多专家建议应该高度重视工作与休闲的平衡, 因为它对幸福成功的生活起着至关重要的作用。

现在许多人不能平衡工作与休闲的一个主要原因是工作量的增加与现代技术的结合使得人们越来越难以在非工作时间切断与工作的联系。那么如何扭转这种局面？也许第一步是人们需要提高工作效率和时间管理能力。具体来说, 确定任务的优先级并且集中注意力在最重要的事情上, 无论在工作中还是家里都是很必须的。 另一个关键步骤是设立工作和休闲时间的边界。最后, 媒体报道有必要使公众注意到工作和休闲之间的平衡。

总之, 我们不应该忽视上面提到的问题，我强烈建议每个人, 特别是年轻人努力在工作和休闲之间取得平衡。
